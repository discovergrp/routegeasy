# Route A Preferred Color Palette (from routeapref.discover-grp.com)

## Key Colors:
- **Primary (Navy Blue)**: oklch(0.36 0.18 240) ≈ #1a3a8f / rgb(26, 58, 143) — used for navbar, hero background, buttons
- **Accent (Gold/Amber)**: oklch(0.78 0.16 85) ≈ #D4AF37 / similar warm gold — used for CTA buttons, highlights
- **Background (Page)**: oklch(0.99 0.005 240) ≈ near white #F8F9FF — used for features, pricing, form sections
- **Text (Dark)**: oklch(0.12 0.04 240) ≈ very dark navy #0d1b3e
- **White**: #ffffff — cards, nav button

## Section Backgrounds:
- **Navbar**: Navy blue (oklch 0.36 0.18 240)
- **Hero Section**: Navy blue (same as navbar, full bleed)
- **Features Section**: WHITE background with navy text
- **Itinerary Section**: Light gray/muted background (oklab 0.96)
- **Pricing Section**: WHITE background
- **Inclusions Section**: Navy blue background
- **Form Section**: White/light background
- **Footer**: Navy blue

## Buttons:
- Primary CTA: Gold/Amber background with dark text
- Secondary CTA: White border, white text (outline style)
- "Book Now" nav button: White background, navy text

## Typography:
- Headings: Dark navy (oklch 0.12 0.04 240)
- Body: Dark navy (same)
- Muted text: oklch(0.45 0.04 240) — medium gray-blue
- Accent text: Gold/amber

## Border/Separator:
- Gold horizontal line under hero title
- Light borders: oklch(0.90 0.02 240)
