# Route A Preferred - Exact Color Palette Reference
Source: https://routeapref.discover-grp.com/

## Primary Colors
- Navy blue (primary/hero/form bg): #1e3a8a (oklch 0.36 0.18 240 ≈ rgb(26,58,138))
- Dark navy (header bg): slightly darker, #0f2460 range
- Accent/gold: #d97706 (amber-orange, oklch 0.78 0.16 85 ≈ rgb(217,119,6))
- Accent light: #f59e0b

## Section Backgrounds
- Hero section: Navy blue #1e3a8a
- Intro card: rgba(white, 0.05) on navy
- Stats cards: rgba(white, 0.07) on navy  
- Features section: Pure WHITE #ffffff
- Itinerary section: Very light gray #f8f9ff or #f1f4fb
- Pricing section: Light gray-white #f8f9ff
- Inclusions section: Navy blue #1e3a8a
- Reservation form: Navy blue #1e3a8a
- Footer: Dark navy #0f2460

## Text Colors
- On dark sections: white #ffffff
- Muted on dark: rgba(255,255,255,0.7)
- On light sections: Dark navy #0d1b3e or #1e293b
- Muted on light: #4a5568 or #6b7a94

## Accent Usage
- Buttons: amber/orange #d97706
- Active tabs: amber/orange #d97706
- Gold divider line: #d97706
- Best seller badge: gradient #f59e0b → #d97706
- Prices in best seller column: #d97706

## Feature Cards (on white section)
- Card bg: white
- Card border: #e2e8f0
- Icon box: navy blue rounded square
- Heading: navy blue
- Body text: #4a5568

## Separator between Hero and Features
- Thin gold/amber line: 3px solid #d97706

## Key Design Notes
- Hero h1: white text, gold italic for tagline
- Section badges: navy bg, white text, pill shape
- Day/city tabs: white bg, navy text; active = amber bg, white text
- Day content box: white bg, light border
- Form inputs: rgba(white, 0.05) bg on navy section
